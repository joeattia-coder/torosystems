Barrier-Free accessibility Solutions in Toronto & the GTA

Toro Systems installs barrier-free accessibility solutions that improve accessibility, convenience, and safe entry for staff, customers, tenants, and visitors. These systems are designed to support easier movement through entrances and shared spaces in commercial and public-facing environments.

Our solutions help businesses create more accessible facilities while improving the day-to-day experience for people entering and exiting the property. Depending on the application, installations can include automatic door operators, accessible entry controls, and barrier-free washroom access components.

We focus on reliable equipment, practical installation design, and systems that are easy to use and maintain over time.
