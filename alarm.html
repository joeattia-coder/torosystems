Commercial Alarm Systems in Toronto & the GTA

Protect your business with a professionally designed commercial alarm system built to detect unauthorized entry, reduce risk, and support a rapid response. Toro Systems installs intrusion alarm systems tailored to your property layout, operational needs, and security priorities.

Our alarm solutions can include wired and wireless components, mobile alerts, remote arming and disarming, and integration with professional monitoring services. Where wiring is difficult or impractical, long-range wireless options can help extend protection without compromising coverage.

With remote access features, owners and managers can check system status, review zones, and manage the alarm from a mobile device. This gives your business greater visibility and control, even when you are off site.
