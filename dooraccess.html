Door Access Controllers

Door access systems provide protection to your buisiness and staff by restricting access to your business only to the people who are granted access.

Door access systems allow you to control the times and days your staff has access to certain areas of your building(s), and control which doors they have access to.

You can achieve the same by using keys, but if a key is lost, you might have to go through the hastle of replacing locks and getting new keys cut for your staff. If a card or fob is lost, you simply disable it and issue a new card to the user.

Remote Access

Lock and unlock doors remotely using your mobile device.

Security

Control who can access any room at any given time, with a wide array of card readers and biometric scanners.

Flexibility

Set schedules for when a card/fob holder can access a specific door.
