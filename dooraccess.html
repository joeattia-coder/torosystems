Commercial Access Control Systems in Toronto & the GTA

Access control systems help businesses manage entry more securely than traditional key-based systems. Toro Systems installs commercial access control solutions that allow you to control who can enter specific doors, areas, and buildings, and when they can do so.

These systems make it easier to assign permissions by user, department, location, or schedule. If a card or fob is lost, access can be revoked quickly without the cost and disruption of changing locks and reissuing keys.

Access control improves both security and day-to-day operations by giving businesses more control over entry, better accountability, and a more flexible way to manage changing staff or facility requirements.
