Commercial Security Systems for Businesses Across Toronto & the GTA

Toro Systems designs and installs commercial security systems for businesses throughout Toronto, the Greater Toronto Area, and Southern Ontario. Our solutions include intrusion alarm systems, CCTV surveillance, access control, and barrier-free accessibility systems tailored to the needs of each property.

We provide complete project delivery, including system design, wiring, conduit installation, equipment setup, programming, and final commissioning. Whether you are securing a small office, retail location, warehouse, condominium, healthcare facility, or multi-tenant property, we help you implement a solution that improves safety, visibility, and control.

An effective security strategy often combines three essential layers: surveillance to monitor activity, access control to manage entry, and alarm systems to detect intrusion and trigger a rapid response. Toro Systems helps clients integrate these systems into one reliable solution built for day-to-day performance and long-term protection.
