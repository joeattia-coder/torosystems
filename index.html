Security Systems and CCTV Installation Services

Toro Systems specializes in the installation and integration of state-the-art intrusion prevention, surveillance, and burglary alarm systems. We provide a complete solution including design, wiring, conduits, installation and programming. To protect your business against intrusion, you need to cover three main areas, monitoring your business with security cameras, preventing unauthorized access with door security solutions, and lastly, detering criminals from breaking in when the business is closed, with a proper alarm solution, and 24/7 monitoring that will alert authorities immediately in case of a breach.

Our technicians are proficient in installing Kantech door access controllers, and DSC security and alarm systems. Kantech and DSC are trademarks of Tyco Security Products, which has the largest security portfolio in the world.
